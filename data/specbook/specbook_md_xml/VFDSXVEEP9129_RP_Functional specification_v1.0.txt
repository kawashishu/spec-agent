14.4.2 Subsystem Description.

The remote parking assist system is developed on the basis of the FAPA system. The system remotely controls the vehicle through C-APP, allowing users to control parking outside the vehicle. This method can effectively solve the embarrassing problem of the driver being unable to enter or exit the vehicle in tight parking spots.

Remote parking has remote parking in function, remote parking out function, and remote straight move function. See the table below for details:

System: The Remote Parking Assist  
Sub System: Remote parking in  
Function Description:  
After the user finds a parking slot while driving the vehicle, the user needs to connect vehicular communication. After the connection is successful, the user can control the vehicle through the C-APP after exiting the vehicle and park the vehicle in the parking space (perpendicular, parallel, angled). The system will take over control of the brake and throttle, steering, and gear shifting.  

Sub System: Remote parking out  
Function Description:  
After the user opens the C-APP and connects the vehicular communication, the vehicle is first remotely started and enters the parking out function. After that, the user can control the vehicle to park from the parking space (parallel, perpendicular, angled) through the C-APP. The system takes over control of the vehicle's power, throttle, steering, and gear shifting.  

Sub System: Remote straight parking  
Function Description:  
After the user opens the C-APP and connects the vehicular communication, the vehicle will be started remotely. After entering the remote straight parking function, the user can control the vehicle to move straight forward or backward through the C-APP.
