**1st row SEAT TRENCHES & QUILTING PATTERN (PLUS).**

The 1st-row seat features a specialized trench design with a depth of 180 mm. Quilting patterns are characterized by diamond shapes, each with dimensions of 59 mm ± 2. The quilting includes perforations, with a diameter of Ø1.2 mm. Perforation spacing is 4.75 mm, and the pattern is arranged at a 45° angle.

Dimensions:
- Quilting square side length: 59 mm ± 2.
- Perforation diameter: Ø1.2 mm.
- Spacing between perforations: 4.75 mm.

These features contribute to a visually appealing and functional cushioning pattern, designed for comfort and aesthetics.

The seat trenches are designed with a dimension of 180 mm. Perforation patterns are arranged in a grid with spacing measurements:  
- 59 mm ± 2 between the center points of perforations, forming a diamond shape.  
- Each perforation has a diameter of Ø1.2 mm.  
- The angle of the diamond pattern is set at 45°.  
- Additional spacing between perforations at 5 mm intervals from edge details.  

These trenches exhibit precision and symmetry essential for maintaining comfort and enhancing visual appeal. The design serves both functional and aesthetic purposes.
