### Diagrams Description

#### MFS-BASE
**Purpose:** Displays the Multi Functional Switch base-level layout intended for SMART/PLUS VN ICE use. Features include gear downshift (IR LED) and cruise control settings.

#### MFS-MID
**Purpose:** Represents the mid-level MFS design for SMART BEV-US/EU/CA/VN vehicles. Includes gear upshift (IR LED) and extended cruise control functionality.

#### MFS-HIGH
**Purpose:** Illustrates the high-level MFS intended for PLUS BEV-US/EU/CA/VN use. Incorporates advanced gear shift controls (IR LED) and additional cruise control features.
