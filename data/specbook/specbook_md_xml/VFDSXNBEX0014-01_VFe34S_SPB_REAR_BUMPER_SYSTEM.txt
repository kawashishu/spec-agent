**1. Component Requirements:**

The component is not allowable for deformation at low and high temperatures. It must perform under all environmental conditions, and its dimensions should remain stable within the range of -40°C to 120°C. The component includes painted and grain parts, with specific colors such as Jet Black and body colors. The grain must meet CMF requirements, and the approved sample must be validated by the CMF team. Painted parts must adhere to relevant VINFAST requirements as detailed in the DVP.

**2. Part Quality Tests:**
- **Climatic Change Test:** VFDST00046100
- **Sunlight Simulation Test:** VFDST00053800
- **Mounting/Dismounting Test:** VFDST00009000
- **Vibration Test:** VFDST00062600
- **Corrosion Test (NSS/Cyclic Corrosion):** ISO 9227/ ISO 11997-B/ ISO 6270 CH
- **Weathering Test:** VFDST00044900
- **Resistance to Chemicals:** VFDST00043900
- **Scratch Test:** VFDST00024801
- **Aging Resistance:** VFDST00053600
- **Paint Quality Test:** VFDST00025600
- **Stone Chip Test:** DIN EN ISO 20567-1 (Acc. to VFDST00044000)
- **Media and Environmental Resistance:** VFDST00025900
- **Color Matching Criteria:** VFDST00038400
- **Appearance Approval Criteria:** VFDST00038401
- **Vehicle Washability Test:** VFDST00009203 (Damage susceptibility of trim parts after washing via roller brush machine)
- **Screw Fastener Tightening Torques:** VFDST00003603

**3. Strength Requirements:**
- **Ultrasonic Welding Retention Test:** VFDST00099000
- **Double-Sided Adhesive Tapes Requirements and Tests:** VFDST00038100
- **CAE Acceptance Criteria:**
  - Natural Frequency Global Mode > 30 Hz.
  - FRA-X & FRA-Z Stress at 33Hz < 7 MPa and Stress at 66Hz < 17.5 MPa.
  - Load Conditions:
    - 49N load (bumper surface): Displacement < 5mm.
    - 98N load (side bumper surface): Displacement < 10mm.
    - 147N load (center bumper surface): Displacement < 9mm.
  - Resistance Against Pressure Washer: VFDST00062800

**4. Durability and Fatigue:**
The component must ensure a mileage target life of 160,000 km or 10 years (whichever comes first) while fulfilling specific system requirements, with compliance to Painted Plastic Parts Standard VFDST00025600.
