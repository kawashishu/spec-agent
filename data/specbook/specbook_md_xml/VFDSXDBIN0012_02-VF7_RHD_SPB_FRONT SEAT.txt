3 Additional Technical or Functional Descriptions.

Refer to Component Drawing and DVP DVPRXDBIN0013.

Feature List:

Model: VF 7S.

Variant: BASE and PLUS.

Drive Style: RHD.

Market Groups (follow homologation types): Group6 Asia RHD, Group5 India RHD.

Feature List Includes:
- Seat capacity: 5.
- Seat material: Vegan leather/Da nhân tạo.
- Driver seat adjustment: 8-way power/chỉnh điện 8 hướng.
- Driver seat memory: No (BASE); Yes (PLUS MY2025).
- Driver Headrest: Adjustable/chỉnh cơ cao thấp.
- Driver seat massage: No.
- Driver seat heated: No.
- Front Pass. seat adjustment: 4-way manual/chỉnh cơ 4 hướng.
- Front Pass. seat memory: No.
- Front Pass. Headrest: Adjustable/chỉnh cơ cao thấp.
- Front Pass. seat massage: No.
- Front Pass. seat heated: No.
- Front Pass. seat ventilation: No (BASE); Yes (PLUS MY2025).
- Front Seat Side Airbag (LH & RH): 2 standard airbags.
- Front Seat Middle Airbag: 0 (MY2025).

Feature specifications are tailored to different market regions and vary between BASE and PLUS variants.
