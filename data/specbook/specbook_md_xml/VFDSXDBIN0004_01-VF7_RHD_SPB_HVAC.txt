### BIN70036501 HVAC_ASSY_AUTO_2Z_R134A_WITH_PTC_PLUS_INDIA_RHD
This image illustrates the HVAC assembly designed for RHD (right-hand drive) vehicles in India. The component features automatic two-zone climate control with R134A refrigerant and integrated PTC (positive temperature coefficient) heating. The design includes enhanced heat-exchange mechanisms while ensuring compatibility with local standards.

---

### BIN70036508 HVAC_ASSY_AUTO_2Z_R134A_WITH_PTC_PLUS_THAI_INDO_RHD
Shown here is the HVAC assembly tailored for RHD vehicles used in Thailand and Indonesia. It includes a two-zone automatic control system with R134A refrigerant, alongside features such as PTC heating for efficient temperature regulation.

---

### BIN71036501 HVAC_ASSY_AUTO_2Z_R134A_WITHOUT_AQS_WITHOUT_IONIZER_WITH_PTC_BASE_RHD
Depicted in this image is the base HVAC assembly for RHD vehicles, featuring automatic two-zone climate control with R134A refrigerant and PTC heating. This model excludes advanced functionalities such as an air quality sensor (AQS) and ionizer, emphasizing basic temperature control functionality for varying environments.
