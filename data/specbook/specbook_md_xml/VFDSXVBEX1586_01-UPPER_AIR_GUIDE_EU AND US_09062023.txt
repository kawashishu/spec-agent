## 2 Specification summary  

### Technical requirements summary  
- **Refer to DVP for Targets and acceptance criteria.**  

#### Material:  
- Refer to 2D drawing: BEX30001100 / BEX30001301.  
- IMDS Requirements.  
- Prohibited and declarable substances: VFDST00007601.  

#### Functional / Performance:  
- Refer to VTS (VFDSXNCVC2802) and 2D drawings.  
- It mounts back to the Front Bumper.  
- No touch noise or squelch noise allowed under any condition.  
- No deformation allowed at low and high temperatures.  
- Components must perform in all conditions, with dimensions stable in the -30 ºC to +80 ºC range.  
- Part grain must meet CMF requirements, and the approved sample should receive CMF team approval.  

#### Part quality:  
- Tightening torques: VFDST00003603.  
- Prohibition and restriction of substances: VFDST00007601.  
- Climatic change test: VFDST00046100 (Mechanical Integration).  
- Resistance to chemicals: VFDST00043900.  
- Media and environmental resistance: VFDST00025900.  
- Recycling and recoverability: UN Regulation No. 133.  

#### Strength:  
- Ultrasonic Welding Retention Test: VFDST00099000.  
- Mounting/dismounting: VFDST00009000.  

### CAE Acceptance Criteria:  
1. Natural Frequency Global Mode > 30 Hz.  
2. FRA-X and FRA-Z Stress @ 33Hz < 7 MPa; Stress @ 66Hz < 17.5 MPa.  
3. Indentation.  
4. Displacement < 8 mm.
</page>

<page number="4">
Durability/Fatigue. The product must ensure a Mileage Target Life of 160000 km or 10 years, whichever comes first, plus specific system requirements. UNPAINTED PLASTIC PARTS VFDST00024801 are required.

Durability/Impact. The product must pass the Climatic Change Test (VFDST00046100), Sunlight Simulation Test (VFDST00053800), and Vibration Test (combined with temperature, VFDST00062600).

Durability/Corrosion. The Fog Test according to ISO6270 CH must be completed.

Safety–Security. Compliance with Thatcham regulations is necessary.

Thermal and Aero. The component must meet PAT targets for aerodynamics and powertrain targets for air intake (thermal performance).

EMC. This requirement is marked as "NO."

NVH (Noise, Vibration, Harshness).  
1. Acoustic and vibrational behavior must fulfill the demands needed to reach complete vehicle targets. The overall behavior must be equivalent to or better than the benchmark.  
2. The system must be acoustically unobtrusive under all work conditions, including rattling, squeaking, tone noise, whine noise, groaning, whistling, actuation-noise, and grinding noise. Sound pressure level and pitch must remain stable without fluctuation (modulation).

Safety. The product(s) or system(s) must comply with applicable regulations, including FMVSS, NCAP, and ECE.

Serviceability. Refer to the Service Manual for details.
