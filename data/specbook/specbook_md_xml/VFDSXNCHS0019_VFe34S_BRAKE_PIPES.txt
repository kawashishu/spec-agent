2 Specification Summary.

**Technical Requirements Summary (1):** Refer to DVP for targets and acceptance criteria.

**Material:** Refer Drawing CHS71011002, CHS71011003, CHS71011022, CHS71011024. Brazed Double Wall Low Carbon Steel Tubing (SAE J527).

**Functional / Performance:** Refer Drawing CHS71011002, CHS71011003, CHS71011022, CHS71011024.
- **Parameter:** Unmensioned bending radius (center line).
  - **Unit:** mm.
  - **Value:** R15.

- **Outside diameter without coating:**
  - **Unit:** mm.
  - **Value:** Ø4.76.

- **Outside diameter with coating:**
  - **Unit:** mm.
  - **Value:** Max. Ø 5.25.

- **Tube thickness:**
  - **Unit:** mm.
  - **Value:** 0.7±0.07.

**Part Quality:** Refer Drawing CHS71011002, CHS71011003, CHS71011022, CHS71011024.
- Marking of parts according to standard VFDST00000010.
- Tube packing: Protection against dust and dirt.
- Material to meet Steel En10084 - 1.1141(C15e).

**Strength:** Refer to DVP DVPRXNCHS0019.
- Tensile strength should be more than 290Mpa.
- Lower yield strength should be more than 200Mpa.
- Elongation before uncoating should be more than 25%.
- Elongation after coating should be more than 18%.
- Hardness should be less than 150HV.

**Durability / Fatigue:** Not relevant.

**Durability / Impact:** Not relevant.

**Durability / Corrosion:** Refer Drawing CHS71011002, CHS71011003, CHS71011022, CHS71011024.
- Product surface treatment: The product should meet neutral salt spray test according to ISO 9227 for the salt spray test time of 720 hrs.
- Corrosion requirements according to ISO standard.
- Corrosion requirements in VFDSXXCVC4401 applicable to project VFe34S.

**Safety – Security:** Not relevant.

**Thermal and Aero:** Refer to DVP DVPRXNCHS0019.

- **Parameter:** Thermal aging.
  - **Unit:** °C.
  - **Value:** Three tubes of 300mm long were taken and the samples were placed in an environment of 150°C for 24h.
  - **Acceptance Criteria:** No peeling or cracking on the coating.
