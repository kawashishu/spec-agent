5. BODY IN WHITE (BIW) PVC SEAM SEALANTS.

Coating requirements:
- **PVC application sizes:**
  - **Type-A:** The thickness of PVC seam is 2-3 mm; the width of seam is 20-25 mm.
  - **Type-B:** The thickness of PVC seam is 1-2 mm; the width of seam is 8-10 mm.
  
- **Starting and ending points:** The starting and ending points are along the edge of sheet metal edge as shown in the figure, and the PVC application position is shown in the figure curve.

- **Operation requirements:** The PVC seam position is as shown in the figure and curve. The application is required to be continuous, complete, and tight without omission, to ensure the sealing performance; the appearance is free of accumulation and burr.

- **Illustrated seams:** Illustrated seams shall overlap on each another (connected).

- **Special note:** There is no PVC residual glue inside and outside the car body, and all kinds of assembly process holes, installation surfaces, and internal and external surfaces of bolts around the weld joints shall be free of PVC. During production, apply PVC, brush flat, and clean the residual material on the appearance surface.

- **Purpose of application:** Sealing against corrosion, waterproof and dustproof.

- **Sealing material:**
  - **Type-A:** EFCOAT PB 289 B1-2 / EFTEC.
  - **Type-B:** TEROSON PV3412 / HENKEL (Used only on DC- & Roof-Lines).

    - **Type-A**: The thickness of the PVC seam is 2-3 mm; the width of the seam is 20-25 mm.  
    - **Type-B**: The thickness of the PVC seam is 1-2 mm; the width of the seam is 8-10 mm.
• The starting and ending points are along the edge of the sheet metal edge as shown in the figure, and the PVC application position is shown in the figure curve.

  The PVC seam position as shown in the figure is required to be continuous, complete, and tight without omission to ensure sealing performance. The appearance should be free from accumulation and burr.

  There should be no PVC residual glue inside or outside the car body.   Sealing against corrosion, waterproofing, and dustproofing.  
• **Sealing Material:**  
  - **Type-A**: EFCOAT PB 289 B1-2 / EFTEC.  
  - **Type-B**: TEROSON PV3412 / HENKEL (Used only on DC- & Roof-Lines).

**Manual Process Guide:**

1. **Process of Brushing**  
   Wiping the adhesive seam with a brush to flatten and clean edges.  

   **Before:** Raised seam before brushing.  
   **After:** Flattened and even seam after brushing.

2. **Process of Skiving/Rubbering**  
   Wiping the adhesive seam with a rubber spatula to achieve a clean and smooth finish.

   **Before:** Raised seam before skiving.  
   **After:** Flattened and smooth seam after skiving.
