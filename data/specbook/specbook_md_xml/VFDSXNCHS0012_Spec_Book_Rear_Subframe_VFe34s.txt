2 Specification summary.

**Technical requirements summary**  
(1) Refer to DVP for Targets and acceptance criteria.  

**Material:**  
Refer to drawing (CHS71018001/04).  

**Functional/Performance:**  
Refer to drawing (CHS71018001/04).  

**Part quality:**  
- Part Simulation  
- Appearance quality  
- Weld joint micro analysis  
- Material Thinning Test  
- Material Validation Test  
- Dimensional Check  
- Compression Test  

**Strength:**  
- Stiffness Measurement – Vehicle X, Y, Z direction.  
- Strength evaluation test – Lower control arm.  
- Strength evaluation test – Connection of Toe link.  
- Strength evaluation test – Connection of camber link.  
- Strength evaluation test – Connection of Body in white – Front and Rear.  
- Bushing Press In & Press Out Tests.  
- Strength evaluation test – Connection of Anti-roll bar.  
- MBD load events for strength.  

**Durability/Fatigue:**  
- MBD load events for durability and tested as an axle system.  

**Durability/Impact:**  
- MBD load events for abuse/extreme events.  
- Tested at the vehicle level.  

**Durability/Corrosion:**  
- Test according to VFDSXXCVC4401.  
  - 720 h NSS (ISO 9227).  
  - 10 cycles CCT (ISO 11997 Cycle B).  

**Safety – Security:**  
NR.  

**Thermal and aero:**  
NR.  

**EMC:**  
NR.  

**NVH:**  
First mode > 140 Hz.
