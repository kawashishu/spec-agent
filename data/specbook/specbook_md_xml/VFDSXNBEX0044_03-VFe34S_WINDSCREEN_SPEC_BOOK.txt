### 2 Specification summary

**Technical requirements summary (1):**  
Refer to DVP for Targets and acceptance criteria.

**Material:**  
Refer to drawing BEX71020004 Windscreen assembly HUD.  
Mixed (glass, rubber…).  
IMDS material data sheets have to be submitted into VinFast IMDS ID according to the official VinFast IMDS Guideline (Testing & Validation) and VFDST00007601. Refer IMDS document for:  
- Recycling & Recoverability - UN Regulation No. 133.  
- Prohibition & Restriction of substances – VFDST00007601.

**Functional / Performance:**  
Refer to drawing BEX71020004 Windscreen assembly HUD.  
- HUD area: The component should be capable of performing in all conditions. The dimensions should be stable through the Range -40°C to 120°C.  
- Flammability (burning behavior) - VFDST00032600 (required for all used Materials).  
  - UN-R118, DIN 75200, FMVSS 302.  
  - Refer DVPRXNBEX0039 (IRVM bracket separate DVP, not included in Windscreen DVP).

**Part quality:**  
Glass defects to be checked by supplier.  
OQ/PQ Requirement.  
RPS & Measuring points.  
Glass defects to be checked by supplier. Additional info in SOR VFSRXNBEX0001.  
Climatic change test - VFDST00046100.  
Vibration (X-axis 8h, Y-axis 8h, Z-axis 12/24h) - VFDST00062600.  
Sunlight simulation - VFDST00053800.  
Parts Marking according to ISO Standards - VFDST00000010 (ISO 1043-1).  
Refer additional information - DVPRXNBEX0044.
