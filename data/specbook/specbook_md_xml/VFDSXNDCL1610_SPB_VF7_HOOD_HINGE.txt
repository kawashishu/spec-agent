### Technical Requirements Summary (1)

#### Material
IMDS document requirements:
- IMDS material data sheets must be submitted into the VinFast IMDS ID according to the official VinFast IMDS Guideline (Testing & Validation) and VFDST00007601.
- Recycling & Recoverability must comply with UN Regulation No. 133.
- Prohibition & Restriction of substances must align with:
  - Vietnam Law No. 06/2007/QH12.
  - GADSL.
  - California Proposition 65.
  - Annex XIV and XVII of Regulation (EC) No 1907/2006.
  - Annex II of Directive 2000/53/EC.

#### Functional / Performance
Refer to DVP DVPRXNDCL1610, SOR VFSRXNDCL1570, and 2D drawings for:
- Dimensional & Profile Tolerances (VFDST00000013).
- Resistance Spot Welding of Steels (VFDST00001002).
- Limit reliability.
- Load test: Loaded with 5000N at a speed of 5mm/min with a fixed arm at 30° inward and 30° outward to a rigid fixture.
- Rotational resistance.

#### Part Quality
- Climatic change test (VFDST00046100).
- Media and Environmental Resistance (VFDST00025900).
- Additional quality requirements listed in DVP DVPRXNDCL1610 and SOR VFSRXNDCL1570.

#### Strength
- Mounting/dismounting: VFDST00009000.

#### Durability / Fatigue
A durability test must be conducted 15,000 times, simulating reciprocating hood opening and closing at the hood's maximum opening angle.

Refer to VFDST00040300 (VinFast Standards) and DVP DVPRXNDCL1610 for corrosion resistance tests.
