### Explore view of RADAR_ADAPTER_REAR_SIDE_LH/RH_FACE_LIFT
This diagram illustrates the structural components associated with RADAR_ADAPTER_REAR_SIDE_LH_FACE_LIFT and RADAR_ADAPTER_REAR_SIDE_RH_FACE_LIFT. Notable parts include:

1. **REAR_BUMPER_CENTRAL_BRACKET_FACELIFT:** The main structural bracket attached to the rear bumper.
2. **REAR RADAR:** A radar device mounted within the adapter for rear-side detection.
3. **RADAR_ADAPTER_REAR_SIDE_RH_FACE_LIFT:** A right-hand radar adapter, identical in structure to the left-hand adapter (LH is the same structure with RH).
4. **HARNESS:** A cable assembly connecting the radar and adapters to the overall system.
5. **SCR:** A screw attachment to secure components.

The LH and RH structures are visually and functionally identical.

3 Additional Technical or Functional Descriptions.

Following approval from CM to do VF9 face lift. These two new parts will be two adapters to assemble the new radars on the current bumper. The bracket might be manufactured by Radar Absorbing Material (RAM) that specifically absorbs at frequencies of 76-81 GHz and includes the functionality of a RAM shield.
