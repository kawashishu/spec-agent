### 3 Additional Technical or Functional Descriptions
The audio amplifier receives the four-way pre-output signal of the multimedia head unit and provides power output signal through the internal DSP processing AMP to the connected speakers. There are some input factors such as vehicle speed, enable mode, and CAN signal needed when processing the sound signal.

- **Audio playback:** 
   - The audio amplifier receives the 04 CH output analog signal from the multimedia head unit. 
   - The amplifier will support 7.1CH with 01 subwoofer.
   
- **Support functions:** 
   - The amplifier will have support functions including ON/OFF/MUTE, sound zone, equalizer, volume control, diagnostics, and flashing via OBD/FOTA.

- **Sound tuning types:** 
   - Entertainment tuning for audio sound. 
   - Announcement tuning for navigation and telephone.

An external amplifier is used in the Plus variant, featuring 11 speakers and 01 subwoofer.
