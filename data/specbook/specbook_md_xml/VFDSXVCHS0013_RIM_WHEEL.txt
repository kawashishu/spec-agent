**Rim marking:**  
Rims must meet the marking requirements according to the Electronic Code of Federal Regulations (eCFR) contained in section S4.4.2. Specifics of marking include:  
- Each rim or disc must be marked with lettering of no less than 3 millimeters in height and must be either impressed to a depth or embossed to a height of no less than 0.125 millimeters.  
- The information listed in S4.4.2(a)-(c) must appear on the outward side of all rims. For multi-piece rims, S4.4.2(a)-(e) must appear on the rim base; S4.4.2(b) and (d) must appear on each part of the rim.  

Information for designations indicating the source of the rim's published nominal dimensions is as follows:  
- (1) “T” indicates The Tire and Rim Association.  
- (2) “E” indicates The European Tyre and Rim Technical Organization.  
- (3) “J” indicates Japan Automobile Tire Manufacturers’ Association, Inc.  
- (4) “L” indicates ABPA (Brazil), aka Associacao Latino Americana De Pneus E Aros.  
- (5) “F” indicates Tire and Rim Engineering Data Committee of South Africa (TREDCO).  
- (6) “S” indicates Scandinavian Tire and Rim Organization (STRO).

The blue dot has to be placed to the design surface for detection in automated tyre assembly.
