3 Additional Technical or Functional Descriptions.

Refer to DVPRXDBIN002.

VF7RHD – INTERIOR CMF SPECBOOK v2 CONSOLE:
- Console 12V Cap: Material defined as "See Explode View," gloss range is 10 ± 2 units, and grain specification matches VF-INT-GS-001. The color is Granite Black (VF-INT-DP-008 / BKB), with an option 1.
- Armrest: Process specifies Wrap Vegan Leather with material variations such as Base (Eco Black), Plus Cotton Beige Eco. Grain options include Granite Black (animal grain) or Cotton Beige (fine grain). Gloss parameters are 1.06 ± 0.2 units or 1.80 ± 0.2 units accordingly, with applicable codes VF-INT-LV-001 and VF-INT-LV-012.
- Lower Rubber Mat: The material is "See Explode View," processed as Mold in color. Gloss tolerance stands at 1.06±0.2 units with a grain match of VF-INT-GS-001 Sand grain and color Granite Black-BKB Option: 1.
- Wireless Charger and USB Port: The USB port material specifications are designated as "See Explode View," processed as Paint with a gloss range of >10 units. Grain defined matches high gloss standards.

VF7RHD – INTERIOR CMF SPECBOOK v2 CONSOLE CUP MATT:
- Cup Holder and Console Bezel: Material follows "See Explode View" processing (MIC no grain + Paint). The grain option matches "no grain" criteria and the paint maintains matt-black properties. Colors align with Granite Black/BKB options.

VF7RHD – INTERIOR CMF SPECBOOK CONSOLE AIR VENT EXPLODE VIEW:
- Air Vent Components: Exploded view illustrates Upper, Lower Spacer (LH/RH), Housing configurations, vertical vanes RH/LH, and forks LH/RH alongside distinctive material grains for MIC technical grain, painted and plating parts. Grain direction denotes vertical and horizontal alignment as specified in technical documentation.
- Material Options: Specifications align with VF-INT-DP-008 granite black/BKB standards and VF-INT-GS grain profiles, as defined for components.

VF7RHD – INTERIOR CMF SPECBOOK v2 CONSOLE AIR VENT:
- Air Vent Finish: Processes utilize mold, color mechanisms adhering to MIC grain profiles. Component esthetics prioritize technical grain orientations.

Figures and diagrams provided visually describe components, assembly processes, material characteristics, grain directions, and technical specifications pertinent to interior cockpit modules.
