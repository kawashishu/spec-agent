The part is tape (Double-sided acrylic foam tape with a polyethylene film liner). The component should be capable of performing in all conditions. The dimensions should be stable. The seals should stay in place to offer a seal surface through the range -40℃ to 120℃.  

Quality of build should guarantee that the seals are fitted as designed to the bumper grille. The design should be robust to allow the seals to perform in all the standard driving conditions.

### 4 Function Requirements:

PILLAR TRIM TAPE is used in the assembly of the exterior trim and ornamentation for VF7 (VFe34S).  

It is mounted on the front and rear doors, bodyside of the vehicle (LH and RH), and has an interference fit with the door system.
