General Items. 

1. This specification is an additional document for the manufacturing plant and is for information only.

2. Structural Adhesive (Inter-Weld Sealer).

Supplier: SIKA  
Material: Sika Power 498/3  

- **Main performance indexes:**  
  Hardness (Shore A): 10-50, shear strength ≥ 20 MPa, failure state ≥ 90% CF.  
  The section size of spot welding sealant: the diameter of the sealant is 3 ± 1 mm, and the distance between the center of the sealant and the edge is 6-8 mm.  
  The initial position of the sealant is at the R angle as the starting point, and the end of the sealant is 3-5 mm from the edge of the sheet metal. A small amount of glue overflow is allowed, with the glue overflow range ≤ 5 mm. A small amount of glue overflow is also allowed in other areas.  

- **Curing conditions:**  
  (140-160) degree centigrade, (30-60) minutes.  

- **Appearance:**  
  Single component black or gray paste, uniform quality, no precipitation, no segregation, no foreign impurities.  

- **Operation requirements:**  
  Glue shall be applied continuously and evenly without breaking, and the width of glue shall be kept uniform. Overlap edge shall be covered to ensure tightness.  

**Function:** Bonding & Sealing.
</page>

2. Structural Adhesive (Inter-Weld Sealer).

Supplier: SIKA.  
Material: Sika Power 498/3.

- **Main performance indexes:** The hardness (Shore A) ranges from 10 to 50. Shear strength is ≥ 20 MPa, and failure state is ≥ 90% CF. The section size of the spot welding sealant has a diameter of 3 ± 1mm. The distance between the center of the sealant and the edge is 6–8mm. The sealant starts at the R angle and ends 3–5mm from the edge of the sheet metal. A small amount of glue overflow is allowed with a maximum range of ≤ 5mm, and this allowance extends to other areas as well.  
- **Curing conditions:** The curing temperature ranges between 140–160 degrees centigrade for a duration of 30–60 minutes.  
- **Appearance:** It is a single-component black or gray paste. The paste is of uniform quality, free of precipitation, segregation, or foreign impurities.  
- **Operation requirements:** Glue must be applied continuously and evenly without breaks. The width of the glue line must remain uniform, covering the overlap edge to ensure tightness.

**Function:** Bonding & Sealing.
