### 4 Function Requirements
DOOR CLADDING TAPE is used in the assembly of the Exterior Trim & Ornamentation for VF7 (VFe34S).  
They are mounted on the FRT_DOOR_CLADDING_LH/RH_PART_BODY. DOOR CLADDING (LH & RH) has an interference fit with the door system.
