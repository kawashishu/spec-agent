**Purpose:** The purpose of these NVH pads is to absorb airborne noise and aid in improved NVH performance of the vehicle.  

**Application:** NVH pads are part of the functional black commodities on the VFe34S model. Noise tests need to be performed at the alpha cabin, generating the performance chart as shown below. This result needs to be confirmed by the NVH engineer.  

**Performance Chart:** The figure illustrates the "Random Incident Absorption Coefficient per SAE," indicating the sound absorption coefficient across different octave band center frequencies.
