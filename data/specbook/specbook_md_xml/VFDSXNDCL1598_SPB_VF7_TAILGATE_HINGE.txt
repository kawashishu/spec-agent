**Material:**

- Recycling and recoverability – UN Regulation No. 133.
- Prohibition and restriction of substances – Vietnam Law No. 06/2007/QH12.
- Prohibition and restriction of substances – GADSL.
- Prohibition and restriction of substances – California Proposition 65.
- Prohibition and restriction of substances – Annex XIV, XVII of Regulation (EC) No 1907/2006.
- Prohibition and restriction of substances – Annex II of Directive 2000/53/EC.

**Functional/Performance:**
- Refer to DVP DVPRXNDCL1598 and SOR VFSRXNDCL1582 for:
    - Function requirements and design considerations such as GD&T datum targets, tolerance zone, spring-back variation, bend radii, load requirements, etc.
    - The hinge should withstand the limit moment of 100Nm without failure.
    - FMVSS 206, ECE R11, and GB15086 tests. Fix hinge in fixture and apply loads as follows in accordance with regulatory requirements:
        - Longitudinal direction: 11kN.
        - Transverse direction: 9kN.
        - Vertical direction: 9kN.
    - The hinge system must not become detached from the fixture or itself.
    - Rotary resistance test: 2Nm of rotating resistance. The hinge must not rotate under its own weight.

**Part Quality:**
- Sunlight simulation – VFDST00053800 (Mechanical Integration).
- Climatic change test – VFDST00046100 (Mechanical Integration).
- Vibration test (combined with temperature) – VFDST00062600 (Mechanical Integration).
- Working temperature: -30°C up to +80°C.

- NA.

**Durability/Fatigue:**
- Refer to DVP DVPRXNDCL1598 for durability test.
- The component must be fully functional over 40,000 lifegate open-close cycles.
