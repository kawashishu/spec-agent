The Car Jack Support is used for the fast lifting of the vehicle. The Car Jack Support is fastened to the vehicle, more precisely, at the existing screw-on points.

- The supplier is responsible for "self-certifying" their part(s), product(s), or system(s) to meet applicable FMVSS/CMVSS before these products can be offered for sale.

- The supplier must notify VinFast of any non-compliance or safety-related defects within five working days of determining its existence.
- The supplier must propose appropriate risk assessments and resolution plans.
