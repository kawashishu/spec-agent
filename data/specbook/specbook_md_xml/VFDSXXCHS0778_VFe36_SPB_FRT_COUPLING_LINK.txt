3.2 Technical requirement.

The supplier is requested to provide an Anti-Roll-Bar (ARB) System, connecting the left and right front axle wheels acting as a torsion spring in order to reduce vehicle roll motion. The ARB is mounted to the front subframe with two brackets and rubber bushings. ARB-Links are connected to the front damper outer tubes. The rear ARB is mounted to the rear subframe with two brackets and rubber bushings. ARB-Links are connected to the rear lower control arm (H-Arm / Swinging Arm).

**Bill of Material:**
- Part Number: CHS80001106
- Part Name: FRONT_COUPLING_LINK
- Quantity: 2
- Material: Not Uniform
- Weight (kg): 0.386

VinFast designated part number, usage per car, procurement code, and the part's applicability for VF36 & VFe36. Technical requirements include part design, part requirements, and functional and performance specifications at the VinFast level. For detailed information, refer to Table 1 and Table 2.

**Figure 1: Front Coupling Link.**

The figure illustrates the Front Coupling Link, which plays a critical role in connecting components within the Anti-Roll-Bar system. This part is essential for ensuring stability and reducing roll motion during vehicle operation.

  Obligated to actively support the Responsible party, including capacity, expenses, and knowledge-sharing.
