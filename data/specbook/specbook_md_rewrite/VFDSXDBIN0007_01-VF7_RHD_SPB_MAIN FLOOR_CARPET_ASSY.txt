3 Additional Technical or Functional descriptions.  
Refer DVPRXDBIN0007.

- Sound absorption coefficient on average of carpets.  
- Absorption on average of the carpet:  

**Sound Absorption Data Table:**  
The table describes the equivalent absorption area of the carpet surfaces under varying frequencies (unit: Hz).  

**C-SUV Target for absorbing carpet (Topside and Backside):**  
- At 400 Hz: 2.52 m² (Topside), 2.59 m² (Backside).  
- At 500 Hz: 2.55 m² (Topside), 2.65 m² (Backside).  
- At 630 Hz: 2.59 m² (Topside), 2.72 m² (Backside).  
... [values continue progressively up to 8000 Hz].  

**C-SUV Target for conventional carpet (felt/foam + heavy layer):**  
- At 400 Hz: 1.33 m² (Topside), 1.28 m² (Backside).  
- At 500 Hz: 1.36 m² (Topside), 1.40 m² (Backside).  
- At 630 Hz: 1.37 m² (Topside), 1.53 m² (Backside).  
... [values continue progressively up to 8000 Hz].
