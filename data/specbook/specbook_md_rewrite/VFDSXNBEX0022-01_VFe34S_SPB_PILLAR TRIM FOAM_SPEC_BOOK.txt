**Part Description:**
The parts are foam (Semi-closed EPDM seal with single side adhesive tape). 

**Performance Criteria:**
- The component should be capable of performing in all conditions. The dimensions should be stable.
- The Seals should stay in place to offer a Seal Surface through the Range -40°C to 120°C.
- Quality of build guarantees that the Seals are fitted as designed to the Bumper grille.
- The design should be robust to ensure the Seals perform in all standard driving conditions.

4 Function Requirements:
Pillar trim foams are used in the assembly of the exterior trim and ornamentation for VF7 (VFe34S). They are mounted on the front & rear doors, bodyside of the vehicle (LH & RH), and have an interference fit with the door system.
