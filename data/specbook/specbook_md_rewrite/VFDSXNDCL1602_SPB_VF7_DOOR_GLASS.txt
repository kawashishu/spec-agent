Technical requirements summary:

Material:
Refer IMDS document for: IMDS material data sheets have to be submitted into VinFast IMDS ID according to the official VinFast IMDS Guideline (Testing & Validation) and VFDST00007601.

Functional / Performance:
Refer to SOR VFSRACBEX1005 for orientation of row panes in glass assembly, glass edges, tolerance allowances etc.  
Operational Strength: The side windows must not break in extreme cases with a door surcharge of 3.2 m/s.  
Light transmission:  
- ECE R43 Rev3 Annex3 Minimum light transmission: 70%.  
- ANSI/SAE Z26.1-1996 Minimum light transmission: 70%.  

Type of glass defects:
- Control condition:  
  The glass is vertically located in front of a black screen illuminated by neon tubes. Inspection is to be made with the naked eye at arm's length.  
- Requirements:  
  Maximum 3 glass defects in Area 1*.  
  Minimum 500 mm distance between two defects.  
  Maximum 2 glass assembly defects in Area 2*.  
  Minimum 500 mm distance between two defects.  
  Maximum 3 enameling defects per part.  
  Minimum 500 mm distance between two defects.  

* Area 1: Transparent area.  
* Area 2: Enameled area.  
* Area 3: Invisible area in assembled vehicle.

Optical reflection:
- Inspection method: Refer to DVP DVPRXNDCL1602.
- Requirements:
  - Waviness, deformations, or sink marks must not appear.
  - A harmonic reflection is required.
- Limiting sample:
  A limiting sample must be defined and agreed with SQA after 3 months of serial production.

Part quality:
- Refer to SOR VFSRACBEX1005 for:
  - Part quality requirements, appearance approval for all visible parts, etc.
- Climatic change test: VFDST00046100.
- Sunlight simulation: VFDST00053800.
- Vibration test (combined with temperature): VFDST00062600.
- Humidity resistance test: Place three 300x300mm samples in a 50°C, 95% R.H. chamber for a duration of 336 hours.
- Boiling test: Place three 300x300mm samples in boiling water for 2 hours.
- Radiation resistance test: Place three 76x300mm samples in a UV chamber for 100 hours.
- Abrasion test:
  - Before abrasion, measure the haze.
  - Test by performing 1000 turns of abrasion on the sample.
  - After abrasion, measure the haze.

Durability / Impact:
- Impact resistance:
  - Mechanical strength test with a 227 g ball.
  - Based on ECE R43 Rev2 Annex 5 Items3 / ANSI/SAE Z26.1-1996.
  - Drop height of ball: 9 m for laminated glass for motor vehicles tested must not break.
- Dart drop test:
  - Use cutting samples placed on support.
  - Raise the dart to 9.14 m and drop to impact the supported sample.
