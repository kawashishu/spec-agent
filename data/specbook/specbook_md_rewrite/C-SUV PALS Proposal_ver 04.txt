### VF Product Attribute Leadership Strategy (PALS)

#### Static items:
1. Design Exterior: L (Leader).
2. Design Interior: L (Leader).
3. HMI: L (Leader).
4. Infotainment & Connectivity: E (Entry) (future), A (Among Leader) in the future with added connectivity features.
5. Durability: L (Leader).
6. Perceived Quality: L (Leader).
7. Interior Environment Quality: L (Leader).
8. Daily Life Usage: L (Leader).
9. Ergonomics: L (Leader).
10. Roominess: L (Leader). Biggest in the segment, approaching D segment with C cost.

#### Powertrain:
11. Performance Feel: L (Leader).
12. Driveability: L (Leader).
13. Transmission Performance: L (Leader).
13. Fuel Economy (Range): L (Leader). Range is better than Tesla.

#### Dynamic items:
14. Steering: L (Leader).
15. Handling: L (Leader).
16. Braking: L (Leader). Safety-related, stopping distance should be A (Among Leader).
17. Ride Comfort: L (Leader).

#### NVH:
18. Road NVH: L (Leader).
19. Wind NVH: L (Leader).
20. Climate Control NVH: L (Leader).
21. Operational NVH: L (Leader).
22. P/T NVH & Sound Quality: L (Leader).

#### Safety:
23. Passive Safety: L (Leader).
24. Active Safety: E (Entry) (future), A (Among Leader).
25. Driver Assistance (ADAS): E (Entry) (future), C (Competitive) (US), C (Competitive) (VN).
    - For the Vietnamese market, ADAS is E-level, C-SUV is C-level, and the future market should be A-level.

#### Comfort & Convenience:
26. Visibility: L (Leader).
27. Seating Comfort: L (Leader).
28. Climate Comfort: L (Leader).
29. Parking Convenience: L (Leader).

#### Users Economy:
30. Purchase Price: L (Leader).

#### Vehicle Safety:
31. Security: E (Entry) (future), A (Among Leader).
