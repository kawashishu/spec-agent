### Grain Types and Definitions

#### **Sand Grain**
- **Material:** PP / ABS / PC+ABS / GF  
- **Grain:** MT-11530 (Sand)  
- **Depth:** 33µm  
- **Draft Angle:** 3.5°  
- **Code:** VF-INT-GS-001  
- **Options:** 2  
- **Gloss:** 1.8G +/-0.2 (Standard Gloss)  
- **Color:** 
  - Black (BKB)  
  - Beige (BGC)  

#### **Technical Grain (Pillar Upper)**
- **Material:** PP / ABS / PC+ABS / GF  
- **Gloss:** 1.8G +/-0.2  
- **Grain:** MT-EG37  
- **Depth:** 78μm  
- **Draft Angle:** 8°  
- **Code:** VF-INT-GT-001  
- **Color:** Black (BKB)  
- **Options:** 1  

#### **Sand Grain (Door’s Triangle)**
- **Material:** PP / ABS / PC+ABS / GF  
- **Gloss:** 1.8G +/-0.2  
- **Grain:** MT-11010  
- **Depth:** 25µm  
- **Draft Angle:** 2.5°  
- **Code:** VF-INT-GS-004  
- **Color:** Black (BKB)  
- **Options:** 1  
- **Note:** MT-11010 will replace TTZ-E1105 Injection Texture  

#### **Animal Grain**
- **Material:** PP / ABS / PC+ABS / GF  
- **Grain:** SETC-011 (Animal)  
- **Code:** VF-INT-GA-001  
- **Color:** Black (BKB)  
- **Options:** 1  
- **Gloss:** 
  - 1.8G +/-0.2 (Standard Gloss)  
  - 1.2G +/-0.2 (Low Gloss Area)  
- **Depth:** 
  - 100µm (No Micro-matte)  
  - 110µm (With Micro-matte)  
- **Draft Angle:** 
  - 10° (No Micro-matte)  
  - 11° (With Micro-matte)  

#### **Sand Grain (Foot Rest)**
- **Material:** PP / ABS / PC+ABS / GF  
- **Gloss:** 1.8G +/-0.2  
- **Grain:** MT-1055-6  
- **Depth:** 45µm  
- **Draft Angle:** 4.5°  
- **Code:** VF-INT-GS-005  
- **Color:** Black (BKB)  
- **Options:** 1
