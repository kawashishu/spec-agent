### Technical requirements summary
1. Refer to DVP for Targets and acceptance criteria.

**Material:**
- Refer to 2D drawing.
- IMDS Requirements.

**Functional / Performance:**
- Refer to VTS (VFDSXNCVC2802) and 2D drawings.
- It mounts on the bumper bracket with clips, and the bumper bracket mounts on the fender with bolts.
- Gap and flush requirements between the fender and bumper need to meet OQ requirements.
- Noise restrictions: No touch noise or squeal noise is allowed under any condition.
- Deformation resistance: The component must not deform at low or high temperatures.
- Capability: The component should perform under all conditions, with dimensions stable across the range -40°C to 120°C.
- Surface requirements: Includes painted parts and grain parts in jet black, body colors, and grain. Must meet CMF requirements, with an approved sample confirmed by the CMF team.
- Painted parts must conform to VINFAST requirements; detailed information is available in the DVP.
