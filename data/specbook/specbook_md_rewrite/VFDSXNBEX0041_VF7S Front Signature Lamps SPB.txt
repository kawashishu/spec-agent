### Additional Technical or Functional Descriptions

#### Description of the Part
The feature list provides useful information. Specific regions used include UK, AUS, SA, JPN, and RHD countries, which should follow a low beam pattern. It identifies if the part is used on ECO or PLUS variants.

#### Functional Requirements
The front signature lamps (comprised of LH and RH) are part of the exterior in the lighting commodity for VF7 (VF34). They are mounted to the front bumper upper panel and to the fender to distinguish the VinFast brand. There is only one variant of the front signature lamp that is used in all available markets, and it is used on both ECO and PLUS variants of VF4.
